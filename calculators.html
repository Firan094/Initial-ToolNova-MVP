How to use these calculators

Use the numbers as a decision helper, not a magic fortune teller.

These calculators are made to help freelancers, students, creators, and small business owners estimate costs, income, and tool spending before making decisions.

Freelance income

Estimate how much you may earn from a project or monthly work. Use it before accepting work so you do not accidentally sell your time like expired bread.

Website cost

AI subscription cost

Estimate how much AI tools may cost monthly. If a tool does not save time or make money, it is probably just a shiny monthly mosquito.

Tip: Always compare the result with your real situation. These are planning tools, not financial advice.
